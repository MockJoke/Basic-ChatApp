Emoji Guide

Use emojis by typing their names enclosed in colons. For example, :hey: will display 👋.

List of emojis

  • 😄 - :)
  • 😞 - :(
  • 👋 - hey
  • 🤣 - lol
  • 👌 - ok
  • 😮 - woah
  • ♥️ - like
